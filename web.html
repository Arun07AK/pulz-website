About PULZ

PULZ

PULZ is a student-led movement dedicated to uplifting lives through AI education. We believe that by teaching students how to use tools like ChatGPT effectively, we can create opportunities, enhance learning, and break the cycle of educational inequality.

Our vision: Every student empowered with AI literacy for a brighter future.

Our Mission

AI Literacy

Teaching students how to effectively use AI tools for learning, problem-solving, and creative expression.

Access

Breaking down barriers to technology education and ensuring every student has the opportunity to learn.

Opportunity

Creating pathways for students to transform their lives through technology and innovation.

Innovation

Fostering creative thinking and problem-solving skills that prepare students for the future.

Our Workshops

Introduction to AI

Students learn the basics of AI and how it can enhance their learning experience.

Hands-on Practice

Interactive sessions where students practice using ChatGPT for various subjects.

Real-world Applications

Exploring how AI can help with homework, research, and creative projects.

Impact Achieved

Reached 100+ students in 2 weeks across multiple schools, with 95% reporting improved learning outcomes.

Top 3 Student-Friendly Prompts

  • Study Helper: "Explain [topic] in simple terms as if I'm learning it for the first time. Include examples."
  • Homework Assistant: "Help me understand this problem step by step: [paste your problem]. Don't just give the answer, teach me how to solve it."
  • Creative Project: "I need to create a [project type] about [topic]. Give me 5 creative ideas and help me develop the best one."
